FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this therapy?

No. OneCoach is not therapy, counseling, or a substitute for professional mental health care. Nash and Reese are AI coaching tools designed for accountability, goal-setting, and personal growth. If you're experiencing a mental health crisis, please contact a licensed professional or call 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).

Why Telegram?

Coaching should meet you where you already are — not in another app you'll forget about. Telegram is fast, private, and available on every platform. No downloads, no logins, no friction.

What happens during the 7-day profile build?

Your coach gets to know you through real conversation — not a quiz. Over 7 days, they'll explore your goals, patterns, what motivates you, and what gets in your way. By the end, they have a deep understanding of how you work and what kind of coaching you respond to.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your conversations are encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your data, never train models on your sessions, and never share your information with third parties. You can export or delete all your data at any time.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel anytime from your account portal at my.getone.coach. Your subscription stays active through the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees, no guilt trips.

What if I go over my hours?

Your coach will give you a heads up when you're approaching your limit. If you go over, extra time is billed at $1.99/hour. You can also upgrade your plan at any time to get more hours.

Who are Nash and Reese?

Nash and Reese are OneCoach's two AI coaching personas. Nash is direct, challenge-first, and built for people who respond to accountability and straight talk. Reese is warmer, more reflective, and designed for those who do their best work with support and encouragement. During onboarding, we'll ask a few questions to match you with the right coach — and you can switch anytime.
